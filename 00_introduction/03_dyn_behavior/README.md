# Dynamic Behaviour of Metal Materials

Welcome to the dynamic behaviour module materials. This directory contains the introductory slide deck focused on the mechanical response of metal materials under dynamic loading.

## Objective

These materials are designed to provide a solid physical and modeling foundation for understanding how metals behave under high strain-rate conditions, such as impact and blast loading. Reviewing these concepts will ensure you are well-prepared for the core topics covered in this course.

## Contents

* **Module 1:** Elasticity  
* **Module 2:** Viscoelasticity  
* **Module 3:** Plasticity  
* **Module 4:** Viscoplasticity  
* **Module 5:** Large deformations  
* **Module 6:** Metal foams  

> **Note to students:** Please review the slide deck before the first session.

## Prerequisites

To get the most out of these materials, a basic understanding of mechanics of materials and continuum mechanics is recommended.
