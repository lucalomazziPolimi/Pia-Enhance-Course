# Basics of Blast Loading

Welcome to the blast loading module materials. This directory contains the introductory slide deck focused on the physics and modeling of blast loads and their effects on structures.

## Objective

These materials are designed to provide a foundational understanding of blast phenomena, including shock wave propagation, pressure loading, and structural response. Reviewing these concepts will prepare you for the simulation and design tasks addressed in the core part of the course.

## Contents

* **Module 1:** Brief introduction to blast loading  
* **Module 2:** Basic shock physics  
* **Module 3:** Blast wave propagation  
* **Module 4:** TNT equivalency and scaling laws  
* **Module 5:** Representation of blast loads  
* **Module 6:** Blast load effects on structures  
* **Module 7:** Modelling approaches  

> **Note to students:** Please review the slide deck before the session.

## Prerequisites

To get the most out of these materials, a basic understanding of mechanics, wave propagation, and continuum mechanics is recommended.
