# Submission Guidelines

## Required Deliverables
You must submit the following two files together by the deadline:

1.  **Lattice STL File**
    *   **Format:** Watertight STL.
    *   **Constraints:** Must fit within a 50x50x50 mm cube and be printable as a single monolithic part.
    *   **Naming Convention:** `<group_name>_lattice.stl`

2.  **Project Report**
    *   **Content:**
        *   The problem as you understood it.
        *   Your design method and the rationale behind your choice.
        *   Predicted results (predicted mass and response).
        *   Reflection: What you would do differently with more time.
    *   **Tone:** Short and structured.

## Submission Deadline
*   **Date:** May 29, 2026.
*   **Important:** Late submissions will **forfeit the physical impact test**. All two files must be submitted together.

## Evaluation Process
Every submitted lattice that meets the deadline will be 3D printed in PA 12 and tested on the low-velocity impact rig at PoliMi under identical conditions (6 kg mass, 50 cm drop).
